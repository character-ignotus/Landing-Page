This project is geared towards making an entire web page using FlexBox. The theme of the web page is coffee-based and every image from the logo image, to the information image and the cards images are custom made by me using AutoCAD.

Different Espresso Drinks
Espresso is made by using high water pressure and finely ground beans to make a small, concentrated shot
Cappuccino is an espresso based drink containing espresso, steamd milk & a thick layer of milk foam.
Late is an espresso based drink containing espresso, steamed milk & a thin layer of foam.
Flat white is an espresso based drink containig espresso & a very thick layer of steamed milk.
